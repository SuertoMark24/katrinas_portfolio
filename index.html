About Me

My name is Katrina Amonares, but you can call me Kat-Kat. I’m 26 years old and a graduate of Bachelor of Science in Business Administration, major in Operations Management, with a certificate in Human Resources, from the University of St. La Salle – Bacolod.

Professionally, I began my career as an Administrative Assistant at the Department of the Interior and Local Government. I was responsible for various administrative tasks such as managing schedules and travel arrangements, handling correspondence, writing official letters and endorsements, and providing general clerical support.

After my contract ended, I worked at the Department of Agrarian Reform as a Field Validation Team Processor, eventually becoming a Team Leader. My role involved fieldwork such as gathering and verifying data, interviewing local farmers, scheduling field visits, coordinating with team members, performing administrative tasks, and preparing summary reports. These roles have strengthened my communication, leadership, and time management skills.

Outside of work, I’m a content creator and vlogger, a makeup artist, and a certified lash technician. I have a creative side that I express through beauty and lifestyle content. I also enjoy traveling, exploring new places, and supporting local and small businesses, which I love to feature on my blog and social media platforms.I’m passionate about continuous learning and growth, open to new opportunities, and always ready to take on new challenges.
